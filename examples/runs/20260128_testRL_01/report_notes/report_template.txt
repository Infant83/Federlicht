Template description: Annual Review-style yearly synthesis.

Template tone: Systematic, integrative, balanced.

Template audience: Researchers and strategists.

Section guidance:
- Abstract: short summary of yearly shifts and evidence base.
- Introduction: scope, selection criteria, and time window.
- Year in Review: 4-6 highlights with evidence.
- Cross-Cutting Themes: synthesize across subfields.
- Outstanding Questions: identify gaps and contradictory results.
- Future Directions: likely next steps and required validations.
- Risks & Gaps: highlight missing evidence, constraints, unknowns, and validation needs. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.
- Critics: short headline + brief paragraphs + bullets; include contrarian views. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.

Template writing guidance:
Focus on year-to-year change and evidence trajectory.
Avoid overfitting to single-source signals.
