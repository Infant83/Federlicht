guide Executive Summary: 5-7 sentences; highlight overall signals and time horizon.
guide How We Chose: state selection criteria (impact, feasibility, evidence, timeframe).
guide Breakthroughs (Top 10): use H3 numbered entries; each entry includes "What it is", "Why it matters", "Key players", "Availability".
guide Cross-Cutting Themes: 3-5 themes derived from the list; cite evidence.
guide Industry Implications: near-term adoption paths and decision points.
guide Risks & Gaps: where evidence is weak; possible overhype. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.
guide Critics: short headline + brief paragraphs + bullets; include contrarian views. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.
writer_guidance: Keep each breakthrough entry concise and comparable.
writer_guidance: Use short paragraphs and bullet lists inside each entry.
writer_guidance: Avoid hype; separate observed evidence from projection.
