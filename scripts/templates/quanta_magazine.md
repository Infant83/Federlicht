guide Lede: 2-3 paragraphs; narrative hook; no bullets.
guide Central Question: 2-4 sentences framing the mystery.
guide The Story So Far: chronological narrative; key papers/actors.
guide How It Works: conceptual mechanism; one equation only if clarifying.
guide Why It Matters: scientific and human implications; cite sources.
guide Open Questions: 3-5 questions; be explicit about uncertainty.
guide Risks & Gaps: highlight missing evidence, constraints, unknowns, and validation needs. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.
guide Critics: short headline + brief paragraphs + bullets; include contrarian views. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.
writer_guidance: Use metaphors sparingly; avoid hype.
writer_guidance: Keep warm, magazine tone without losing rigor.
