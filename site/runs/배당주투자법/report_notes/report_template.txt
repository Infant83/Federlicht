Guidance:
Template description: ACS-style review article structure.

Template tone: Scholarly, chemistry and materials focus.

Template audience: Materials science and chemistry researchers.

Section guidance:
- Abstract: 5-7 sentences; scope and key conclusions.
- Current Landscape: categorize approaches and evidence.
- Mechanistic Insights: explain underlying mechanisms.
- Applications: highlight real-world use cases and gaps.
- Challenges: unresolved issues and data limitations.
- Outlook: expected advances and validation needs.
- Risks & Gaps: highlight missing evidence, constraints, unknowns, and validation needs. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.
- Critics: short headline + brief paragraphs + bullets; include contrarian views. If not applicable, state Not applicable and give a brief reason; scale depth to evidence strength and context.

Template writing guidance:
Use clear terminology and define acronyms.
Separate consensus from speculative signals.
